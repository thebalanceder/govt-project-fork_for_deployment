# 📘 CSPOPS - Simple Explanations Guide

## For Prime Minister & Government Officials

---

## 🎯 What is CSPOPS?

**CSPOPS** = **C**itizen **S**entiment & **P**ublic **O**pinion **P**erception **S**ystem

**In Simple Words:**
> A dashboard that tells you **what people are thinking** and **how they feel** about government policies, using AI to analyze news, social media, and economic data.

**Think of it like:**
- 📊 A "weather forecast" for public opinion
- 🧠 An AI assistant that reads thousands of articles for you
- 🚨 An early warning system for emerging crises

---

## 📋 Dashboard Sections Explained

### Tab 1: 📊 Dashboard (Main Overview)

**What It Shows:**
- Key metrics at a glance (like a car's dashboard)
- Quick view of economy, sentiment, and crises

**Why It Matters:**
- **Busy officials** can see everything in 30 seconds
- **Red flags** are immediately visible
- **Trends** show if things are getting better or worse

**Key Metrics:**
| Metric | What It Means | Good Value | Bad Value |
|--------|---------------|------------|-----------|
| 10-Year Treasury | Cost of government borrowing | 2-3% | >5% |
| Unemployment Rate | % of people without jobs | <4% | >6% |
| News Articles | Media coverage volume | Normal | Spike (crisis) |
| Active Crises | Emergencies being tracked | 0 | >2 |

---

### Tab 2: 📈 Economy (Economic Indicators)

**What It Shows:**
- Real-time economic data from Federal Reserve
- Trends showing where economy is heading
- Forecasts based on historical patterns

**Why It Matters:**
- **Economic problems** show up here first
- **Policy decisions** need economic context
- **Public mood** often follows economic trends

**Key Indicators Explained:**

| Indicator | Simple Explanation | Why PM Cares |
|-----------|-------------------|--------------|
| **DGS10** (10-Year Treasury) | Interest rate for 10-year government loans | High rates = expensive borrowing = slower growth |
| **DGS2** (2-Year Treasury) | Short-term government borrowing rate | Reflects Fed policy expectations |
| **UNRATE** (Unemployment) | % of workforce without jobs | High unemployment = public unhappiness |
| **CPIAUCSL** (CPI/Inflation) | How fast prices are rising | High inflation = cost of living crisis |
| **VIXCLS** (Volatility Index) | Stock market "fear gauge" | High = market uncertainty |

**Trend Projection Explained:**
```
What happened: 4.30% → 4.35% → 4.38% → 4.40% → 4.42%
                    ↓
AI predicts:  4.42% → 4.45% → 4.48% → 4.51% → 4.54%
```
- **Blue line**: What actually happened
- **Green dashed line**: Where it's heading

### Tab 3: 💬 Sentiment (Public Opinion Analysis)

**What It Shows:**
- News articles from last 24 hours
- Social media discussions
- Word frequency (what topics are trending)
- AI-powered emotion detection

**Why It Matters:**
- **Early detection** of public anger/fear
- **Policy feedback** in real-time
- **Media narrative** tracking

**NLP Analysis Features:**

1. **Sentiment Analysis** (AI reads text, determines positive/negative)
   - ✅ "Great policy!" → Positive
   - ❌ "Terrible decision!" → Negative

2. **Emotion Detection** (AI identifies emotions)
   - 😠 Anger: "This is outrageous!"
   - 😨 Fear: "I'm worried about..."
   - 😊 Joy: "Excellent news!"
   - 😢 Sadness: "This is heartbreaking"

3. **Named Entity Recognition** (AI finds people, orgs, places)
   - "Federal Reserve" → Organization
   - "$4.42%" → Money
   - "March 2026" → Date

4. **Topic Modeling** (AI discovers main topics)
   - Topic 0: Economy (jobs, unemployment, inflation)
   - Topic 1: Healthcare (policy, insurance, reform)
   - Topic 2: Education (schools, students, funding)

### Tab 7: 🤖 AI Insights (NEW!)

**What It Shows:**
- 💬 AI Chatbot (ask questions)
- 📋 AI Summary Reports
- 🕸️ Cause-Effect Graph

**Why It Matters:**
- **Natural language** queries (just ask!)
- **Automated summaries** (save time)
- **Visual relationships** (see connections)

**AI Chatbot Examples:**

**You ask:** "What does DGS10 mean?"
**AI answers:** "DGS10 is the 10-Year Treasury Rate - the interest rate the US government pays to borrow money for 10 years. Current rate is 4.42%. Higher rates can slow economic growth."

**You ask:** "What's the public mood?"
**AI answers:** "Overall sentiment is positive (65% positive, 15% negative). Dominant emotion is joy (35%). However, anger levels are rising in healthcare discussions."

**You ask:** "What should I focus on?"
**AI answers:** "Based on the data:
1. Address healthcare costs (high anger detected)
2. Communicate economic successes (sentiment is positive)
3. Monitor unemployment (trending upward)"

**Cause-Effect Graph Explained:**

```
[🔵 DGS10: 4.42%] ──influences──→ [🟢 Sentiment: +0.65] ──triggers──→ [🟣 Emotion: Joy]
                                          ↓
                                    causes alert
                                          ↓
                                    [🟡 ⚠️ High treasury yields]
```

- **Circles (Nodes)**: Economic indicators, sentiment, emotions, alerts
- **Arrows (Edges)**: Show relationships ("influences", "causes", "triggers")
- **Colors**: 🔵 Economic, 🟢 Sentiment, 🟣 Emotion, 🟡 Alert

## 🧠 AI Features Explained

### 1. AI Chatbot (DeepSeek/OpenRouter)

**What It Is:**
- An AI assistant trained on your dashboard data
- Can answer questions in natural language
- Provides policy recommendations

**How To Use:**
1. Get API key (DeepSeek or OpenRouter)
2. Add to `.env` file
3. Type questions in chat box

**Example Questions:**
- "What's the unemployment trend?"
- "Explain why sentiment is negative"
- "What policies do you recommend?"
- "Compare current data to last month"

**Cost:** ~$0.01-0.10 per query (DeepSeek pricing)

---

### 2. AI Summary Reports

**What It Is:**
- Automatically generated executive summaries
- 3 paragraphs: Current state, concerns, recommendations
- Written in clear, professional language

**How To Use:**
1. Click "📄 Generate Executive Summary"
2. Wait ~10 seconds
3. Read or copy the summary

**Example Output:**
```
CURRENT STATE:
Overall public sentiment is positive (65% positive, 15% negative). 
Economic indicators show stable growth with 10-year treasury at 4.42% 
and unemployment at 4.4%.

KEY CONCERNS:
Rising anger levels detected in healthcare discussions (32% anger). 
Treasury yields have increased 0.12% this week, potentially signaling 
inflation concerns.

RECOMMENDATIONS:
1. Launch targeted communication on healthcare policy
2. Prepare economic messaging if treasury yields continue rising
3. Monitor unemployment trends for early intervention
```

---

### 3. Cause-Effect Analysis

**What It Is:**
- Visual graph showing relationships between factors
- Identifies what's causing what
- Helps predict downstream effects

**How To Read:**
```
[Factor A] ──causes──→ [Factor B] ──triggers──→ [Factor C]
```

**Example Insights:**
- "Rising DGS10 → Falling sentiment → Rising anger"
- "Low unemployment → Positive sentiment → Joy dominant"
- "High inflation alerts → Economic concern → Fear rising"

**Why It Matters:**
- **Identify root causes** (not just symptoms)
- **Predict chain reactions** (if X happens, then Y)
- **Target interventions** (fix the cause, not the effect)

### When To Check Dashboard

| Situation | Check This Tab | What To Look For |

| **Morning briefing** | Dashboard | Red flags, key metrics |
